About Us

Over the next few years there will be a growth in people who want to be more “green”, more self-sufficient, and take part in the “grow local” movement.  A handful of egg-laying hens in a relatively small yard allows people to participate in these trends without having to move or change their lifestyle.  ChickenInfo.com promotes the green, self-sufficient, and grow-local movements by educating people on how to raise chickens properly. As the content of the site grows, so will the questions from visitors... lots of questions. Everything from hatching eggs to how to raise chickens. We will try to answer as many questions as we could by adding information to the site, but we realize there are too many different variables to create content for every situation. The growth in the trend toward raising backyard chickens and the quantity and quality of information gathered on this site will hopefully exceed any of the original expectations.

Enjoy your time here! If you have comments or questions, turn to the Contact Us it's a great way where you will get LOTS of great answers to any questions you may have.
